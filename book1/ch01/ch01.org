* Getting Started

[[https://doc.rust-lang.org/book/ch01-00-getting-started.html][Link]]

** Basic info

*** Installation

Linux or macOS

#+begin_src

$ curl --proto '=https' --tlsv1.2 https://sh.rustup.rs -sSf | sh

#+end_src

*** Checking installation

#+begin_src

$ rustc --version

#+end_src

*** Update

#+begin_src

$ rustup update

#+end_src

*** Uninstall

and

#+begin_src

$ rustup self uninstall

#+end_src

*** Local copy of documentation

#+begin_src

$ rustup doc

#+end_src
